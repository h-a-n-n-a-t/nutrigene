About NutriGene

❓ What is NutriGene?

NutriGene is a genetic mutation identification and personalized nutrition recommendation system. It uses a questionnaire-based approach to identify potential genetic mutations and provides tailored dietary recommendations based on your genetic profile.

📊 How Accurate Is This?

This tool provides preliminary genetic risk assessment based on symptom analysis. For definitive genetic testing, please consult with a licensed geneticist or healthcare provider.

🧬 Supported Genes

Currently, NutriGene can analyze mutations in 16+ genes related to:

  • Type 1 and Type 2 Diabetes
  • Obesity and Metabolism
  • Lactose Intolerance
  • Cholesterol and Cardiovascular Health

🔒 Data Privacy

Your responses are processed securely and not stored permanently unless you consent to research participation.
